# DawnChat Trademark Policy

This policy describes how third parties may use DawnChat names, logos, and brand assets.

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- Word marks: DawnChat, 晓话 AI
- Product names, project names, and taglines used by DawnChat
- DawnChat logos, icons, and visual brand assets

## Allowed Uses

You may use DawnChat marks only for nominative and descriptive purposes, such as:
- Referring to this open-source project factually
- Linking to the official DawnChat repository or website
- Stating compatibility, for example "works with DawnChat"
- Reporting news, tutorials, reviews, and technical analysis

These uses must be truthful, non-misleading, and must not imply endorsement.

## Prohibited Uses

You may not, without prior written permission:
- Use DawnChat marks in your product name, app name, company name, or domain name
- Use DawnChat logos, icons, or trade dress in a way that can cause brand confusion
- Present forked or modified builds as official DawnChat releases
- Use DawnChat marks in advertisements that imply sponsorship, partnership, or certification
- Register marks, social handles, or domains that are confusingly similar to DawnChat

## Forks and Redistributed Builds

If you distribute modified versions:
- You must use a distinct name, icon, and branding
- You must clearly state that your build is unofficial and modified
- You must not use official DawnChat logos as your primary app icon or identity

## Official Assets

Unless explicitly stated otherwise, DawnChat brand assets are not granted under the software license.
Use of trademarks is governed by this policy, not by the Apache-2.0 license.

## Enforcement

We may request changes or removal of violating uses to protect users from confusion and fraud.
Non-compliant uses may be subject to legal enforcement where applicable.
